21-Day Challenge

Simple daily activities to build your fitness habit

Day 1 15 min

Foundation Walk

5-minute brisk walk + 10 minutes of basic bodyweight exercises (squats, push-ups, stretches)

Moderate Beginner
Day 2 12 min

Stair Climb

Climb stairs for 45 seconds, walk down for rest. Repeat 6-8 times depending on your pace.

Vigorous Cardio
Day 3 20 min

Active Recovery

Gentle stretching + slow walk around campus. Listen to music or a podcast while moving.

Light Recovery
